Who are we?

ECESTORMS (Electrical and Computer Engineering Society for Toronto Metropolitan Students) is a student society run by Electrical and Computer Engineering students for their peers. ECESTORMS conducts multiple events that promote professional development and a sense of community.

What we do?

ECESTORMS's primary responsibilities include representing undergraduate students at the department level, providing a discussion forum for concerns and feedback, and coordinating social and educational events to promote interaction and networking among engineering students. ECESTORMS organizes and holds events throughout the year, some of these events include guest speakers from the industry, professionals from the engineering profession, resume-building seminars, and promoting national engineering week.
