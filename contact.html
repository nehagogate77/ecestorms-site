At ECESTORMS, our mission is to serve as the premier student group for electrical and computer engineering undergraduates at Toronto Metropolitan University. We aim to provide events and services that cater to the professional, academic, and social needs of our members, with a focus on fostering a strong sense of community among students already attending Toronto Metropolitan University. Through our initiatives, we strive to equip our members with the skills, resources, and opportunities needed to excel in their careers, while promoting the values of excellence, leadership, and integrity in all that we do.
